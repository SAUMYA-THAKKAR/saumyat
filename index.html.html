About Free Fire India

Free Fire is a popular battle royale mobile game in India, offering fast-paced 10-minute matches with 50 players dropping into a remote island. The game has gained massive popularity due to its exciting gameplay and frequent updates.

In Free Fire, players fight to be the last one standing, either as solo players or in teams of up to 4 players. With new events and challenges every season, Free Fire continues to engage millions of players across India.
